How to Play 13 Sheep

Goal: Protect all 13 sheep by surrounding them with fences!

Game Rules:

  • Roll the dice to get a fence shape
  • Choose orientation by clicking the shape options below the dice
  • Place fences by clicking on the board - hover to see preview
  • Protect sheep by completely surrounding them with fences
  • You have 10 rolls to protect all sheep

Important Restrictions:

  • Fences cannot overlap or intersect with existing ones
  • You cannot place fences on bushes (dotted lines with ðŸŒ³)
  • Bushes do not protect your sheep

Winning:

  • Protect all 13 sheep to win!
Tip: Try to create efficient enclosed areas that can protect multiple sheep at once! Use the shape reference guides on the sides to plan your strategy.
